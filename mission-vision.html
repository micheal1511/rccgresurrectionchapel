OUR MISSION & VISION

Our Guiding Mission

We are called to fulfill God's purpose through clear objectives that guide our ministry and outreach.

1

To make heaven

Our primary goal is to attain eternal salvation through faith in Jesus Christ and living according to God's will.

2

To take as many people with us

We are committed to evangelism and discipleship, bringing others to the knowledge of Christ for their salvation.

3

To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations

We aim for global impact, reaching every family across all nations with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

4

Holiness as Our Lifestyle

To accomplish our first mission, we commit to holiness as our daily lifestyle, living in obedience to God's word.

5

Strategic Church Planting

We will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing and developed countries.

Our Ultimate Vision

We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Commitment

Through prayer, evangelism, discipleship, and church planting, we are dedicated to fulfilling the Great Commission until Christ returns.

Biblical Foundation

All our mission and vision statements are rooted in Biblical principles and teachings.

Prayer Driven

We believe in the power of prayer to accomplish God's purposes through our ministry.

Community Focused

Our mission extends to serving and transforming communities through Christ's love.

Generational Impact

We are building a legacy of faith that will impact generations to come.
